About us

Carrigan Silage is a family owned business operated by Andrew Carrigan and family. We are based on the Darling Downs and have been producers of silage since the late 80's.

We bought our first self propelled forage harvester in 1992, a Claas 690 which was able to cater for our lot feeding operations.

During the late 80's and early 90's very little information was available regarding the ensiling process, however the experience gained during this time remains invaluable to us.

We are also consumers of silage, feeding up to ten thousand ton each year. We have grown the crops, harvested them and then fed the silage through our own feedlot.

We are coming from the same side of the fence as our customers and this is why we continue to measure our performance by the quality of our product.

Services

Carrigan silage can provide a range services operating throughout QLD, NSW and Victoria including:

  • Silage Contracting
  • High Moisture Grain Making
  • Swathing/Windrowing
  • Silage Pushing/Rolling
  • Bulk Haulage, Silage, Grain etc.
  • Wet Weather Paddock Haul Out
  • Supply of Plastic and Inoculant
  • Pit Design
  • Feedout Advice

Carrigan silage provides a smooth and timely operation that prides itself on communication with the client at all stages of the job from pre-plant advice, pit design, harvest, storage and feedout. Being silage users we understand the precise needs of farmers:

  • Feeders of Silage that understand the needs of other silage feeders
  • Timely and efficient operation on time every time
  • Clients needs are our number one priority
  • Proven silage making history

Equipment

Carrigan silage operates a range of high performing and efficient, modern machinery to provide customers with a reliable and highly efficient service utilising the latest in silage making technology and equipment. Carrigan Silage currently runs:

  • Claas 940 forage harvester with kernel processor
  • 6 mtr Claas corn front
  • Claas pick-up front
  • Fendt 920 push tractor with buck rake/blade
  • Case 9280 push tractor with blade
  • Honeybee 7.5mtr draper front
  • 1 x Volvo prime mover with printer for scales
  • 2 x Moore chassis tippers with scales and road train dolly
  • 1 x Volvo 8 wheeler body truck
  • Subcontractor with flotation tyres on body trucks for wet weather

Carrigan Silage knows it takes more than just a bunch of machinery to make high quality silage and often performs its own research and development at home to keep up with the latest trends and products.
